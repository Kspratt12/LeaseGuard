COMMERCIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Lease Agreement ("Lease") is entered into as of January 15, 2024, by and between
GREENFIELD PROPERTIES LLC ("Landlord") and SUMMIT RETAIL GROUP INC. ("Tenant").

SECTION 1 — PREMISES AND TERM

Landlord hereby leases to Tenant and Tenant hereby leases from Landlord the demised premises
located at Suite 240, 500 Commerce Drive, Springfield, IL 62704 (the "Premises"), consisting of
approximately 4,200 rentable square feet within the Building containing approximately 52,000
rentable square feet of leasable area.

The Lease Commencement Date shall be March 1, 2024. The term of this Lease shall be five (5)
years, expiring on February 28, 2029, unless sooner terminated in accordance with the terms
hereof. Tenant shall have one (1) renewal option for an additional five (5) year term.

SECTION 2 — BASE RENT

Tenant shall pay Landlord base rent of $7,350.00 per month ($88,200 annual rent), payable in
advance on the first day of each calendar month. Base rent shall increase by 3% annually on
each anniversary of the Lease Commencement Date.

SECTION 3 — OPERATING EXPENSES AND CAM CHARGES

3.1 Pro-Rata Share. Tenant's pro-rata share of Building Operating Expenses shall be 8.08%,
calculated as 4,200 / 52,000 rentable square feet.

3.2 CAM Cap. Controllable Operating Expenses shall not exceed a 5% annual increase over the
prior calendar year's actual controllable operating expenses ("CAM Cap"). For purposes of this
section, controllable expenses exclude property taxes, insurance, utilities, and snow removal.

3.3 Administrative Fee. Landlord may charge an administrative fee not to exceed 15% of total
operating expenses for management and oversight of the common areas.

3.4 Excluded Expenses. Notwithstanding anything to the contrary, the following categories shall
not be included in Operating Expenses charged to Tenant:
  (a) Capital improvements or capital expenditures, except to the extent amortized over the
      useful life of the improvement with interest at 8% per annum;
  (b) Structural repairs to the roof, foundation, or exterior walls;
  (c) Depreciation or amortization of the Building (other than as permitted above);
  (d) Leasing commissions, brokerage fees, or tenant improvement allowances;
  (e) Environmental remediation costs, including asbestos or mold remediation;
  (f) Debt service, mortgage payments, or interest on Landlord's financing;
  (g) Costs of any litigation not directly related to Building operations.

3.5 Audit Rights. Tenant shall have the right, at Tenant's expense, to audit Landlord's books
and records related to Operating Expenses within 120 days of receiving the annual reconciliation
statement.

SECTION 4 — SECURITY DEPOSIT

Tenant shall deposit with Landlord a security deposit of $14,700.00, equal to two months' base
rent.

SECTION 5 — PERMITTED USE

The Premises shall be used solely for general retail and office purposes consistent with the
permitted use under applicable zoning regulations.

SECTION 6 — TRIPLE NET PROVISIONS

This Lease is structured as a triple net (NNN) lease. Tenant shall be responsible for its
pro-rata share of property taxes, building insurance, and common area maintenance charges,
subject to the limitations set forth in Section 3.
