OFFICE SPACE LEASE

Between: Pacific Plaza Associates (Owner) and Bright Solutions LLC (Occupant)

Dated: August 1, 2023

Premises: Unit 5B, Pacific Plaza, 2200 Harbor Blvd, Costa Mesa, CA

The Occupant agrees to lease approximately 2,800 sq ft on the fifth floor.
The building contains roughly 40,000 sq ft total.

Rent: The monthly amount payable is $5,600 (annual rent $67,200).

Operating Cost Sharing: Occupant is responsible for their proportionate share
of building operating costs based on occupied area. Tenant's share: 7.00%

Building Costs: Operating costs include but are not limited to taxes, insurance,
maintenance, repairs, landscaping, security, and utilities.

Increases: Annual increases in controllable operating costs are limited to
no more than 4% over the prior year. Taxes and insurance are excluded from this limit.

Property Management: The Owner may engage a property manager. The manager's fee
shall not exceed 12% of operating costs collected.

Items Not Chargeable to Occupant:
The Owner shall not pass through to Occupant any costs related to:
- Capital replacements or major structural work
- Roof or foundation repairs of a capital nature
- Environmental cleanup or hazardous material remediation
- Legal disputes between Owner and other tenants
- Mortgage or financing costs
- Tenant buildout or improvement allowances for other occupants

The Occupant may review the Owner's expense records annually.

This agreement is binding upon execution by both parties.
